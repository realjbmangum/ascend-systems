- Brand-kit.md (#1C1C1E charcoal, #C45A2C orange) and the live site theme
  in `src/styles/globals.css` (#1E2A32 charcoal, #D4632C orange) DIFFER.

  the brand-kit doc.

  for email instead.

  Gmail's render pass. The v1 email used flex-style div nesting; v2 was
  rewritten as nested `<table>` elements.
