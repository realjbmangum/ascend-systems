**[Knock/walk into business]**

> "Hey, how's it going? I'm Brian — I'm local, I work with a bunch of businesses around [area]. Quick question for you: how many calls a day do you think go to voicemail when you're busy or closed?"

**[Let them answer — usually "a few" or "I don't know"]**

> "Yeah, most business owners don't know. The national average is 62% of calls get missed. And here's the kicker — 85% of those people never call back. They just call whoever picks up first."

**[Pause — let it land]**

> "I built a tool that fixes that. It's an AI assistant that answers your phone 24/7, sounds like a real person, and books appointments right into your calendar. Takes 15 minutes to set up. Want to hear what it sounds like? I can call your own demo line right now."

**[If yes — do the live demo. If no — leave the pitch sheet.]**

## Demo Transition Script

After the opener, when they say "sure, show me":

> "Okay, here's what I'm going to do. I've set up a demo assistant with your business name. I'm going to call this number right now on speakerphone, and you're going to hear exactly what your customers would hear. Ready?"

**[Call the demo number. Let AI answer. Let them listen for 15-20 seconds.]**

> "Pretty wild, right? That's answering your phone right now. After hours. Weekends. When you're on a job. And every time it answers, it texts you the caller's info and books the appointment."

**[Transition to pricing]**

> "Plans start at $199 a month. No contract. For most businesses, one extra job pays for the whole month. Want me to get yours set up today?"

## Top 10 Objections & Responses

### 1. "I can't afford $199/month."

> "I hear you — every dollar matters. Here's how I look at it: if you miss just one call that would have been a $300 job, you've already lost more than the monthly cost. CallSteady isn't an expense — it's the cheapest employee you'll ever have. Works 24/7, never calls in sick, and costs less than one job per month. Plus there's a 30-day money-back guarantee, so there's literally no risk."

### 2. "I already have a receptionist / answering service."

> "That's great — and I'm not trying to replace anyone. But let me ask: who answers at 8pm on a Tuesday? Saturday morning? During lunch? CallSteady covers the gaps your current setup can't. A lot of our clients use us alongside their receptionist — she handles business hours, we handle everything else. The calls you're missing aren't during the day. They're nights and weekends."

### 3. "My customers won't want to talk to AI."

> "I thought the same thing before I heard it. Here's what I've found: your customers don't care whether it's AI or a person. They care that someone picked up, listened, and booked them. That's it. And our AI sounds so natural that most people don't even realize. But you don't have to take my word for it — call this number right now and see if you can tell."

### 4. "I'm not good with technology."

> "Neither are most of our clients — and that's the whole point. You don't install anything. You don't log into anything. You don't manage anything. We set it up, you get text messages when someone calls. That's it. If you can read a text, you can use CallSteady."

### 5. "I need to think about it."

> "Totally fair. While you're thinking, though, consider this — every day without it is another day of missed calls going to voicemail. I'll leave you my card and this info sheet. But I'd love to at least set up your free demo so you can hear what it sounds like. No commitment, just a listen. Can we do that?"

### 6. "I don't miss that many calls."

> "You might be right — but here's the thing: you don't know what you don't know. When a call goes to voicemail and the person hangs up, there's no record of it. No missed call notification for the ones that come through on a busy line. Most business owners who start with us are shocked when they see the actual numbers. First week, they usually say 'I had no idea.' Want to find out?"

### 7. "What about my regulars? They want to talk to ME."

> "And they still will! CallSteady only answers when you can't. If you're available, you pick up like normal. We're the backup, not the replacement. Your regulars reach you when you're free. New customers reach us when you're busy. Everybody wins."

### 8. "AI is going to mess up and make me look bad."

> "Fair concern. Here's the thing — your AI is trained specifically on your business. It's not winging it. It knows your services, your hours, your pricing, your service area. And if a question comes in that it's not trained on, it doesn't guess. It takes a message and texts you immediately. Plus, you can listen to every single call recording. If something ever doesn't sound right, we fix it same day."

### 9. "I had a bad experience with an answering service before."

> "I hear that a lot, actually. Traditional answering services hire $12/hour workers who don't know your business and put callers on hold. That's a completely different thing. CallSteady answers instantly — no hold time ever. And it's trained on YOUR business, not reading off a generic script. It's like having your best employee answer the phone every single time. But I get the skepticism — that's why we have the 30-day guarantee. If you're not blown away, full refund."

### 10. "Let me talk to my wife / partner / business partner."

> "Absolutely — big decision, makes sense to talk it over. Here's what I'd suggest: let me set up a demo line with your business name so you can both call it tonight and hear it together. No charge, no commitment. That way you're not describing it — they can experience it firsthand. Deal?"
